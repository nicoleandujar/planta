Wachuma
San Pedro Ceremony

San Pedro
This Ancestral ritual is one of the lesser known nowadays in the Andes. The Wachuma ceremony, also known as San Pedro, is directed by an Andean priest or Medicine Man. Before the ceremony, the medicine man prepares this medicine, accompanied with prayers and melodies from an array of native instruments. The best of prayers and intentions become part of this medicine for those who will arrive to the ceremony and participate in this sacred encounter.

Welcome!

Welcome to P LA N T A , a resource about the wonderful world of Ayahuasca, Peyote, San Pedro, and Psilocybin Mushrooms where we explore the healing, creative, cultural, traditional and scientific aspects of these entheogen. Explore Plant Medicines if you are new to the subject. Take a look at our Resources to explore how you can help and serve the medicine community. And check out the Workshops for continued education from wisdom keepers all over the world.
